Scholarships for Ambitious Women

Explore funding options that support girls, students, and women professionals to continue education, research, and career growth.

Top Opportunities

These scholarships offer financial support, mentorship, and access to higher education for women across different stages of life.

Previous Next

Pragati Scholarship

Designed for girls in AICTE-approved diploma or degree programs, this scholarship offers up to INR 30,000 to help cover education expenses.

Savitribai Phule Scholarship

Supports backward-class girl students in Maharashtra from Class 8 to 10 so they can continue their studies without financial pressure.

DST Women Scientist Fellowship

Provides up to INR 40,000 per month for women scientists who have taken a career break and want to re-enter research and development.

Indira Gandhi Scholarship

Offers financial help to single girl children pursuing postgraduate studies in non-professional courses, encouraging continued higher education.

Kotak Kanya Scholarship

Supports meritorious girls from underprivileged backgrounds after 12th grade with funds to continue their higher education.

Scholarship search tips

Check eligibility and application deadlines early, keep your academic documents ready, and use government portals or institutional websites to apply for funding that fits your goals.
