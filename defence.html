Self-Defence Skills Every Woman Should Know

Practical moves, quick thinking, and confidence can make all the difference in a dangerous moment. Learn strong, smart actions and use them when it matters most.

Powerful Tips & Techniques

These simple self-defence actions are easy to remember and are designed for everyday safety, whether you’re walking alone, traveling, or in a stressful situation.

Arm Grab Escape

If someone grabs your wrist, twist your arm toward their thumb, step back, and move your body away. This breaks the hold and creates space to escape.

Ground Defence Basics

Learn how to stay safe if you are pushed down. Protect your head, use your legs to create distance, and strike vulnerable targets on your way up.

Back Grab Escape

Drop your weight, shift your hips, then step or spin out while pushing backward. A strong knee or palm strike helps break the hold quickly.

Commit to Action

Once you decide to defend yourself, move decisively. Hesitation gives the attacker time to increase force; staying focused keeps you safer.

Everyday Safety Habits

  • Stay aware of your surroundings and trust your instincts.
  • Carry a charged mobile phone and share your route with someone you trust.
  • Choose well-lit, populated routes and avoid distractions like headphones.
  • Practice self-defence moves regularly so they feel natural when you need them.

Stay alert, stay prepared, and stay safe.
